Cape Verde

The greatest mission there is!

Fun Facts

  • It is a country made up of 10 islands and 1 is uninhabited
  • It is the third largest nesting site for loggerhead turtles
  • The island of Sal was once a center for salt mining
  • The island of Boa Vista is a breeding ground for humpback whales

The Church in Cape Verde

The Church of Jesus Christ became official in Cape Verde in 1990 and their temple was recently dedicated in June of 2022. I had the privelege of being able to be there for the temple dedication and serve there from 2022-2023
